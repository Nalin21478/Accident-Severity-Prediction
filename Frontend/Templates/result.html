Did You Know?

  • On average, there are over 6 million car accidents in the United States each year.
  • Weather conditions contribute to a significant number of accidents, so always be cautious in adverse weather.
  • Most accidents occur within 25 miles of home, emphasizing the importance of safe driving in familiar areas.
  • Distracted driving, including texting and using smartphones, is a leading cause of accidents.
  • Wearing a seatbelt significantly reduces the risk of injury and death in a car accident.
